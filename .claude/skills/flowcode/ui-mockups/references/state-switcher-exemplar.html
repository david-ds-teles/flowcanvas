Pricing page rewrite

A focused read of the same item — the reader is the same content rendered for distraction-free review, not a different document.

Summary

Replace the legacy two-column pricing layout with a three-tier comparison table, an annual/monthly toggle, and a short FAQ. Copy is approved; the remaining work is wiring the grid to the live plan matrix.

Why now

The current page converts poorly on mobile and can’t express the new usage-based team tier. The rewrite unblocks the Q3 launch messaging and the metering rollout.

Acceptance

  • Annual toggle recomputes every cell without a reload
  • Feature rows source from plans.matrix.json — no hand-kept copy
  • FAQ collapses on mobile and passes the §11 contrast floor

Rejected alternative

A single “contact us” tier was rejected — it hid pricing and tested worse in the pricing survey. The three-tier grid keeps every plan legible at a glance.
