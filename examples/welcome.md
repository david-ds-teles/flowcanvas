## What you are looking at

Every card on this board is a **real file**. Flowcanvas reads markdown from disk, turns each file's
frontmatter into the compact table above, and renders the body right here in the node.

## Try it

- Drag a card to move it around.
- Click the **–** in a card header to collapse the body to a frontmatter-only chip.
- Open your own board with `?path=your-board.canvas` (relative to the project root).
